Ingredients:
 - 26oz can of SpaghettiOs
 - 1 lb of ground beef
 - Greek Seasoning
 - Garlic Powder
 - Parmesan Cheese
 
 Directions:
 1. Brown the ground beef in a large skillet
 
 2. Season with Greek Seasoning and Garlic Powder
 
 3. Add SpaghettiOs. Let cook until SpaghettiOs boil.
 
 4. Remove from heat, ready to eat!
